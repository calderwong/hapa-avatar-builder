# Public demo fixture boundary

The public package ships a deliberately bounded content volume rather than an empty shell or the operator's complete local libraries.

## Included

- Red, Blue, and Green as three compact public Avatar Cards with repository-authored abstract portraits.
- Six profile loadout Cards per Avatar: three Protocol Cards and three Skill Cards. These are the minimum attached records required for coherent RGB profile displays.
- Three original text-only **Echo State** preview Song Cards: `Red Signal`, `Blue Return`, and `Green Horizon`. No audio, stems, source sessions, or private lyrics are bundled.
- Five sampled Cards in each foundation family visible in the Items UI: Protocol, Skill, and Node.
- The 18 profile-required loadout Cards in addition to those samples, producing 14 Protocol, 14 Skill, and 5 Node Cards in the public Item store.
- The complete 16-card **Codex Build Week** Wisdom Set: four Turn, four Skill, four Capability, and four Protocol Cards. Its 16 Krea-generated proposal images are included with their source-fixture, hash, review, non-mint, non-canon, and non-identity-truth metadata.
- Four deterministic Stargate test Cards generated by source code for the judge route.
- An empty Ballad of Bella placeholder and a small three-lane Kanban bootstrap required for clean compilation.

## Excluded

- The other 71 local Avatar Cards and their private minds, relationships, media, journals, and histories.
- The full song catalog, source audio, stems, render projects, lyrics corpus, and song-reference graph.
- The hundreds of RGB Tarot/song/item attachments that are not required to render the compact public profiles.
- The remaining Tarot and Item libraries, generated media, local model weights, local databases, P2P secrets, Gate Passes, credentials, and machine-local runtime state.

The tracked stores live under `fixtures/build-week/judge-data/`. A clean checkout reads them only when the corresponding local-first store under `data/` does not exist. The first explicit edit writes to the normal ignored runtime store; it never mutates the tracked public fixture in place.

The package builder excludes all of `data/**`, `artifacts/**`, third-party reference images under `public-static/media/`, the older sample reference board, dependency folders, and prebuilt app bundles. It includes only the curated assets under `public-static/demo/`.

This is a review-stage demo volume. Visibility does not imply mint, canon, endorsement, identity authority, ownership transfer, or commercial eligibility.
